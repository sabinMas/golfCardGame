How to Play Each Turn

  1. Draw the top card from the draw pile. You may then replace any one of your cards in front of you with the card you drew.
  2. Instead of drawing from the draw pile you may draw the top card from the discard pile. You may replace one of your cards with it or, if you decide against using it, return it to the discard pile before replacing any cards.
  3. Rather than drawing, you may flip over one of your own face‑down cards that has not yet been revealed.
